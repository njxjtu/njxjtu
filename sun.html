Welcome to the Open Source University Meetup (OSUM) Community


Introduction


OSUM (pronounced "awesome") is a global community of students that are passionate about Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) and how it is Changing (Y)Our World. We call it a "Meetup" to encourage collaboration between student groups to create an even stronger open source community. The "Main" tab above displays the global view for Members and Groups. Once you select any of the other tabs in the horizontal navigation bar, such as "Group", you can use the pull-down menus to browse OSUMs by university or by country. Have fun!

TOP 5 Reasons to JOIN OSUM:
LEARN about Open Source Technologies and how they are opening up new
career opportunities for students
ACCESS free online web courses, webinars, tutorials and other resources to
prepare for career enhancing certifications
SHARE your technical knowledge with other students around the world
virtually and on campus
COLLABORATE with a global community of students via forum, campus
events, webinars, etc.
CONNECT and make friends with students around the world who are
committed to Open Source just like you!

Sign Up for Open Source University Meetup!
1. Direct your browser to: http://osum.sun.com
Note: We support Internet Explorer 6 & 7, Firefox 2 & 3 and Safari 3.0. If you're using a different
browser (perhaps one provided by your internet service provider) it might not be compatible. Make
sure cookies and JavaScript are enabled on your browser.
2. Click on ''Sign Up'
3. Enter your e-mail address, a password and your birthday date and code4. Click
once again on ''Sign Up''5. Select your language and login in with your e-mail
address and password.
JOIN Duke UNIVERSITY OSUM GROUP!
Now that you are a member of OSUM, JOIN Duke University OSUM GROUP
1. Using the pull down menu bar, select your country location, select your school(Duke University )
2. Using the Horizontal Navigation bar, select – Groups
3. Search and select Duke University OSUM
Select JOIN THE GROUP
Congratulations! You are now part of Duke University OSUM
